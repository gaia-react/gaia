# Chromatic Opt-Out

Ask Claude to walk through the opt-out steps. The procedure involves uninstalling `chromatic`, deleting the GitHub workflow and `.storybook/chromatic/` folder, and updating `.storybook/preview.ts` to use `WrapDecorator` directly.
